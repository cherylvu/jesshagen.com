ABOUT JESSICA HAGEN

With eagerness and excitement, Jessica has entered the field of real estate. After moving to Philadelphia in 1998 from Irmo, South Carolina, Jessica open and worked in many restaurants such as Bar Noir and The Royal Tavern. Jessica then turned her hobby into a career and started working for Advanced Sports/Fuji Bicycles. Jessica is an avid cyclist and still very active in the bicycle community.

Jessica first became interested in real estate as a teen while watching her maternal grandfather and paternal grandmother being top agents in South Carolina. Always interested in architechure, Jessica's father grew up in Charleston, South Carolina where to this day you can tour some of the finest, oldest homes in the nation. Growing up she would walk around Charleston admiring the homes.

Professionally, Jessica is known for her integrity and dedication, and her ability to maintain long and sincere relationships. She is familiar with the various Philadelphia and suburban neighborhoods. She is a life-long learner committed to acquiring the knowledge needed to close the deal. Her enthusiasm is contagious.

Jessica currently lives in East Mt. Airy in the home she purchased with her husband.
